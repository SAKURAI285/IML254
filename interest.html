My Personal Interest: Avalon le Fae

Avalon Le Fae is a sub story or arc in a game called Fate Grand Order. This is my favourite arc in Fate/Grand Order. In total, this arc lasted around 36 hours which is only the duration of story and it's not including gameplay time. I still remember spending about one to two weeks finishing it… but hey, it was totally worth it, I guess! The story, world-building, and emotional depth really stood out to me. This arc also stands out from the others sub-storyin this game in terms of visuals, map design, and writing. (I guess the developers really love Arthurian legend, huh...)



Why I Love Avalon le Fae So Much...

Map & Visuals

The map of Avalon le Fae is one of the reasons why I love this arc so much. It’s so beautiful to me, and I think it's the best map I’ve seen in Fate/Grand Order.

My Favourite Characters

Morgan

She’s been my favorite character throughout my entire time playing this game. At first, I assumed she was just another villain, but after finishing Avalon le Fae, I realized how wrong I was about her. What I love most about Morgan is her striking character design and the fact that she has a tragic backstory. Honestly…she deserves so much better.

Oberon

This Mothman is one of the main reasons why Avalon le Fae is the best sub-story in Fate/Grand Order. The way his character shifts from being an ally to becoming the final boss in the story makes him one of the most iconic villains in the entire game.

Castoria

She’s the heart and soul of this story and the true main character. What draws me to her the most is her unwavering determination. No matter how many times she stumbles or falls, she never gives up on what she believes in. And honestly, that kind of strength… it really inspires me...

The Story

Avalon le Fae is honestly the best sub-story or arc I’ve read/played so far. Besides having an amazing plot, every character from the main cast to the side characters has their own unique charm. I didn’t even skip the story here, which is kinda different from the earlier arcs in Fate/Grand Order heheh. A lot of FGO fans also agree that Avalon le Fae is the best arc so far and I remember someone literally say “Avalon le Fae is when FGO peaked” and I'm totally agree with him and i think that’s just facts. From the plot, the characters, the story build-up, all the way to the ending — it was perfect to me. Oh, and below are some of my favorite moments from this arc while I was playing.
