My Family

My Grandmother

My Grandmother name is Nismah Binti Said. She's someone that looks fierce outside but warm inside. She always gives me motivation and also supports me financially. And the funny thing is, my siblings always say that I’m our grandma’s favorite grandchild. But honestly, I feel like she treats all of us the same (well, that’s just my opinion though — I’m not sure what the other grandkids think).

My Father

My dad’s name is Izam Azali Bin Ishak and he’s 57 years old. He works as a teacher at a school Puchong Utama 2. He’s honestly such a funny guy and always cracking jokes when we’re feeling down. And yeah, we all enjoy being around him (even if his jokes can be kinda lame sometimes hehe..). My Mother

My mum’s name is Norhaslinda Binti Ibrahim and she’s 52 years old. She’s a teacher at a school at Taman Desaminium, but fun fact — she actually used to teach me Malay back when I was in SK Puchong Batu 14! She really loves her kids a lot. She’s also been a big help financially (along with my dad) while I’ve been studying here.
